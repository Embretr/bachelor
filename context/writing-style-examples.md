## Phrases That Work in This Thesis

> A list of sentence starters and transitions that match the formal, impersonal academic register.

**Opening claims:**
- "The interviews indicate that..."
- "It can be argued that..."
- "The results suggest..."
- "A consistent finding across all seven interviews was..."

**Connecting to theory:**
- "This aligns with the human-in-the-loop design principle described in Section 2.2..."
- "As [Author] ([Year]) argues, [claim], a pattern that is evident in..."

**Hedging appropriately:**
- "While the sample is limited to seven companies..."
- "This finding should be interpreted with caution given that..."

**Avoiding:**
- "We believe..." → "it can be argued"
- "We found..." → "the interviews reveal" / "the analysis shows"
- "Obviously..." → remove entirely
- "It is interesting that..." → state why it is significant, not that it is interesting
