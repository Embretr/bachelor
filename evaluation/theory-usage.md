## Status legend

- **planned** — theory is in outline, not yet written
- **connected** — theory introduced in Ch 2 and referenced in later chapter(s)
- **orphaned** — theory introduced but not used in any later chapter (flag for removal or connection)
- **missing** — theory used in later chapter but not introduced in Ch 2 (flag for addition)
